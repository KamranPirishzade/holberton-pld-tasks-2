Pokémon, short for "Pocket Monsters," is a globally beloved franchise that originated in Japan. It is created by Satoshi Tajiri and Ken Sugimori, and captures the imagination of millions with its unique blend of adventure, friendship, and the pursuit of becoming a Pokémon Master. In this fictional world, trainers embark on journeys to capture, train, and battle creatures known as Pokémon. Each Pokémon possesses its own unique abilities, characteristics, and types, allowing for strategic battles and endless combinations.
